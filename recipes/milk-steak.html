Milk steak with jelly beans

Description

This exotic dish comes from the genius mind of a man from Philadelphia

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Milk
  • Jelly Beans

Steps

  1. Cook the steak for a certain amount of time (I'm not Gordan Ramsey).
  2. Pour milk onto a plate.
  3. Put cooked steak onto said milk.
  4. Sprinkle some jelly beans on top.
  5. Enjoy.
